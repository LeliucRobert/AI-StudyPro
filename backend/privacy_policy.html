Privacy Policy

Welcome to AI StudyPro, an extension designed to assist users in learning and understanding web content with AI-based features. We value your privacy and aim to be transparent about how we collect, use, and process data.

Purpose of the Extension

The purpose of AI StudyPro (the "Extension") is to enhance your learning experience by providing AI-driven assistance for summarization, explanations, and finding similar pages related to the content you are viewing.

Data Collection

In order to provide a personalized and effective experience, we collect certain information from the web pages you visit, including:

  • Page Title: We collect the title of the web page you are on to give context to the AI for generating relevant responses, and also to search for similar pages.
  • Page Content: The text content of the web page is collected to allow the AI to generate summaries, explanations, or similar page recommendations.

Data Usage

The collected data (page title and content) is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Generate Summaries: Summarizing long texts into simpler, more concise explanations.
  • Provide Related Pages: Offering suggestions for similar pages for further learning and exploration.
  • Contextual AI Responses: Providing contextually relevant AI responses based on the page's content.

Data Storage and Security

We do not store any personal data or browsing history. The data collected is processed temporarily for generating the response and is discarded immediately after the task is complete. All interactions with the AI are secured to protect your information.

User Consent

By using AI StudyPro, you acknowledge and agree to the data collection and processing practices described in this policy.
